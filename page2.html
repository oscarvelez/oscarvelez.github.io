History of Chevrolet Camaro

The Camaro is manufactured by Chevrolet, classified as a Pony car, but Muscle models are also available


Model years range from 1967-2002 when production ended, and 2010-present


The Camaro was reported to be a competitor at the time to the Ford Mustang


One of the most famous Camaro in popular culture can be seen in Transformers Bumblebee




First Generation

Debuted in in 1966, the model was available until 1969.

Second Generation

Introduced in 1970, and produced until 1981

Third Generation

This generation was in production from 1982-1992

Fourth Generation

From 1993-2002, Chevrolet ended the production of Camaros after this

Fifth Generation

The comeback came with a redesigned model from 2010-2015

Sixth Generation

On the 50th anniversary in 2016 Chevrolet introduced the latest model
