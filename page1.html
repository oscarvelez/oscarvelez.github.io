History of Ford Mustang

Ford Mustang is an American car manufactured by Ford , starting in 1964.


Since 1964, the Ford Mustang has had several different platforms and designs over the decades, with different models, classes, and body styles.


To date, there have been 6 different generations, and different models including:


  • Mustang GT
  • Shelby Mustang
  • Mach 1
  • Boss 302
  • Boss 429
